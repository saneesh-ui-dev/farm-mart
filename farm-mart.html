Farm Mart Story of Success & Growth

Having been in business for many years, Farm Mart has gained expertise and insight. Our expertise in the industry has given us a deep knowledge of customer needs and how to meet them. Our longevity in the market is a testament to our product quality and customer satisfaction.

The story of Farm Mart spans Generations

With basic business knowledge and training from India, Mr Davees Paulose, the managing director of Farm Mart, started his entrepreneurial journey in 1983 with a fruits and vegetables wholesale and retail business in Saudi Arabia & later expanded to UAE. In 2006, he founded Farm Gold, exporting fresh fruits and vegetables from India. Later, in 2011, he launched Farm Mart/Farm Gold, a retail store in Ponnurunni and subsequently expanded to other locations in Kochi, including Panampilly Nagar and Kakkanad.

Now Farm mart has decided to upgrade to a larger platform to give customers a satisfying shopping experience and is planning to launch 10000 sq ft supermarkets across Kerala. In addition to launching new supermarkets, Farm Mart is accommodating other Lines of Business (LOBs) in the area to reflect as a business hub, in order to effectively meet customer needs across all segments.

We have already launched a new supermarket in Padivattom, Kochi. It is over 10000 sq ft and has a variety of items, from essentials to pantry staples, including local food products such as bananas. We are proud to support local farmers by buying from the Vegetable and Fruit Promotion Council Keralam (VFPCK), providing our customers with fresh, locally-sourced produce.

In the next five years, we are looking to expand our retail presence in major places in Kerala. At Farm Mart, we are proud to have nurtured a legacy of quality and trust for over forty years. We believe in the power of collaboration and are inviting franchisees to join us.

Vision

By the end of 2023, our goal is to have established a network of stores in various places in Kerala. We want to expand and improve our customer service throughout Kerala, with plans to establish our own farms in various locations in South India. This will enable us to offer a wide range of healthy and wholesome products at our stores, ensuring that our customers have access to safe and hazard-free goods.

Mission

At Farm Mart ,we care about you and what you consume. We always ensure only the best get stocked on our store shelves. We are committed to offering customers across India access to fresh, locally sourced produce while promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting local farmers.

Our mission is to cultivate a stress-free and enjoyable shopping atmosphere where customers, employees and stakeholders alike feel welcomed, fulfilled, and rewarded. We are devoted to providing quality products, exceptional customer service, and great value for money to our customers, and we will continue to do so for generations to come.

Our goal is to create a memorable shopping experience that exceeds expectations and fosters a sense of community and satisfaction.
